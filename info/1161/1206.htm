PhD Candidate

Ph.D. Positions
  • 1. Eligibilities & requirements

    (1) Highly motivated and self-driven for research excellence;

    (2) Ability to communicate/work effectively with PI, team members, and collaborators;

    (3) Hold or expect to obtain a bachelor’s or master’s degree in materials science and engineering, metallurgy, electronic/thermal/chemical engineering, or a related field. Westlake University uses an application-review scheme for recruiting Ph.D. students for applicants holding a bachelor’s degree or a master’s degree.

    (4) Evaluations will be based on research capabilities and potential, not on alma mater. Publication experience (particularly as a first author) in peer-reviewed journals is preferred.

    (5) Compliance with Ph.D. admission policies and requirements of Westlake University.

    (6) Both domestic and international students can apply.

  • 2. Things to know before application

    (1) Evidence of English language proficiency: Applicants must meet at least one of the following requirements: CET-6 score ≥ 460 or TOEFL score ≥ 90 or IELTS score ≥ 6.0 or received a bachelor's or master’s degree from an institution in which the official language of instruction is English (Certificate of Foreign Academic Qualifications and Degrees issued by the Chinese Service Center for Scholarly Exchange should be provided before enrollment, joint-degree programs between Chinese and foreign institutions are excluded).

    (2) Application procedure: Westlake University Ph.D. programs have two admission rounds for international applicants and three admission rounds for domestic applicants. The application process includes an online application and an interview. Interviews are conducted by a faculty committee. For specifics please refer to the Ph.D. admissions webpage of Westlake University.

    (3) Number of positions available: Our lab usually takes 2–3 Ph.D. students per year. Prospective applicants are strongly encouraged to contact PI prior to lodging a formal application.

    (4) Stipend and conference support: The University and lab provide living stipends for Ph.D. students. The lab provides financial supports for Ph.D. students to attend domestic/international conferences.

  • 3. Laboratory space and facilities

    (1) Dedicated lab and office space;

    (2) State-of-the-art lab equipment and facilities;

    (3) Collaborative environment and diverse culture;

    (4) Institutional/national/international network and collaborations.

  • 4. How to apply

    Interested applicants should submit the following materials (as one PDF file) to jianbotang@westlake.edu.cn with the subject reading “Ph.D. Application-Applicant Name”. Only shortlisted candidates will be informed and interviewed. A formal application must be submitted via the online application system for Ph.D. programs of Westlake University.

    (1) CV;

    (2) Academic certificates (bachelor’s/master’s degree) and transcripts;

    (3) Evidence of English proficiency;

    (4) A copy of publication(s) and other related material (if any).
